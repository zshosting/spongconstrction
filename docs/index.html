Process

Spong Construction works together with architects, interior designers, and on-site to ensure the coordination of all the project team members. We conduct an initial site evaluation, oversees the estimating and value engineering process, scheduling, construction and final completion of the home.

Over the years, we have worked with many of the finest architects, engineers, interior designers, and lighting consultants in the country. We can assist you with the selection of any of these project team members. We are equally particular about our subcontractors, most of which we have worked with and relied upon for years. The same holds true for our vendors and suppliers. We work only with professionals who share our commitment to excellence. Single-source responsibility: it installs confidence and peace of mind for you while building your custom home.

Communication is essential, especially if you are not present during the construction period. We conduct frequent cost-tracking and progress meetings. We also provide progress pictures, schedules, and work completed information regarding your project. This information is sent directly to you.
